The Solar System

Discover the planets of our solar system

Mercury

Gravity: 3.7 m/s²

Diameter: 4,880 km

Mass: 3.3 x 1023 kg

Position: 1st from the Sun

Type: Rocky planet

Atmosphere: Very thin, mostly oxygen.

Special Features: Smallest planet; extreme temperature changes; no moons.

Rotation Period: 59 Earth days

Revolution Period: 88 Earth days

Venus

Gravity: 8.87 m/s²

Diameter: 12,104 km

Mass: 4.87 x 1024 kg

Position: 2nd from the Sun

Type: Rocky planet

Atmosphere: Thick, mostly carbon dioxide.

Special Features: Hottest planet; known for its thick clouds; no moons.

Rotation Period: 243 Earth days (retrograde)

Revolution Period: 225 Earth days

Earth

Gravity: 9.8 m/s²

Diameter: 12,742 km

Mass: 5.97 x 1024 kg

Position: 3rd from the Sun

Type: Rocky planet

Atmosphere: Nitrogen and oxygen.

Special Features: Supports life; has liquid water; diverse ecosystems.

Rotation Period: 24 hours

Revolution Period: 365.25 days

Mars

Gravity: 3.71 m/s²

Diameter: 6,779 km

Mass: 6.39 x 1023 kg

Position: 4th from the Sun

Type: Rocky planet

Atmosphere: Thin, mostly carbon dioxide.

Special Features: Known as the Red Planet; has the largest volcano and canyon in the solar system; evidence of past water.

Rotation Period: 24.6 hours

Revolution Period: 687 Earth days

Jupiter

Gravity: 24.79 m/s²

Diameter: 139,820 km

Mass: 1.90 x 1027 kg

Position: 5th from the Sun

Type: Gas giant

Atmosphere: Mostly hydrogen and helium.

Special Features: Largest planet; famous for its Great Red Spot; has over 75 moons.

Rotation Period: 9.9 hours

Revolution Period: 11.86 Earth years

Saturn

Gravity: 10.44 m/s²

Diameter: 116,460 km

Mass: 5.68 x 1026 kg

Position: 6th from the Sun

Type: Gas giant

Atmosphere: Mostly hydrogen and helium.

Special Features: Known for its stunning ring system; has over 80 moons.

Rotation Period: 10.7 hours

Revolution Period: 29.46 Earth years

Uranus

Gravity: 8.69 m/s²

Diameter: 50,724 km

Mass: 8.68 x 1025 kg

Position: 7th from the Sun

Type: Ice giant

Atmosphere: Hydrogen, helium, and methane.

Special Features: Rotates on its side; has rings and 27 known moons.

Rotation Period: 17.2 hours

Revolution Period: 84 Earth years

Neptune

Gravity: 11.15 m/s²

Diameter: 49,244 km

Mass: 1.02 x 1026 kg

Position: 8th from the Sun

Type: Ice giant

Atmosphere: Hydrogen, helium, and methane.

Special Features: Farthest planet; known for strong winds; has 14 moons.

Rotation Period: 16.1 hours

Revolution Period: 165 Earth years
